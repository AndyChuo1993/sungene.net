# SunGene 可宣稱事實清單 (Source of Truth)

**Purpose**: 任何人(包括 LLM)寫 sungene.net 的 site copy / articles / schema /
marketing 文案前,先讀本檔。對應每一條 trust signal,確認在 ✅ 區才寫。

**Last updated**: 2026-05-21 (**v2.0**, Phase 2 brand reposition — "Taiwan-based
trading company" voice; Pinion v1.2 "sourcing partner / margin transparency"
voice retired)

**Rule of thumb 一句**:
> 寫任何 trust signal 之前先問——「這條客戶想驗證,他能去哪裡看到第三方證明?」
> 看得到 → 可寫(✅ 區)
> 看不到、只能自己說 → 不能寫(❌ 區)
> 不確定 → ⚠️ 區,要老闆 case-by-case 確認

## 🎯 Brand voice(v2.0 核心)

SunGene 的對外身份是 **"Taiwan-based trading company supplying selected
products through manufacturing coordination and export management across
Taiwan and China."** 寫文案時請維持這個 supplier 主體,**不要寫成**:

- sourcing partner / sourcing agent / 採購夥伴 / 採購代理 / 採購顧問
- QC company / 驗貨服務 / 品檢平台
- factory-direct / direct factory / 工廠直供 / 工廠直接
- consulting / sourcing consulting / 採購顧問
- dual-entity procurement / 雙公司採購服務

正確語氣:

| 寫成 | 不要寫成 |
|---|---|
| "We supply X" | "We help you source X" |
| "Quotation from us" | "We find you a factory" |
| "Shipment by us" | "We connect you with the factory" |
| "Coordinated by us" | "We're a transparent middleman" |
| "Quality checked by us" | "We provide QC service" |
| "供應" / "出口" / "整合生產" | "幫你找工廠" / "幫你驗貨" / "幫你公開成本" |

**為什麼**:supplier voice 跟 broker/agent voice 客戶感受差一個世界。前者買產品,
後者付服務費。後者會被「為什麼不直接找工廠」這個問題擊穿。

### Operational capabilities

- **Manufacturing coordination**: SunGene 與台灣與中國的 selected manufacturing
  partners 配合做生產、品質、出貨協調。寫 marketing copy 用「manufacturing
  coordination」/「製造協調」/「coordination manufacturière」/「coordinación de
  fabricación」,不寫「sourcing」/「採購」當主動詞。
- **Dedicated in-house QC staff**(verified by owner):SunGene employs
  full-time QC personnel based at the China office who travel to partner
  factories for on-site pre-shipment AQL inspection. NOT subcontracted to
  third-party inspection companies(QIMA / Bureau Veritas / TÜV etc.)。
  **寫法**:可以寫「quality checked by us」「on-site QC by SunGene staff」,
  但不要在 Hero 一直喊 QC。QC 是 backing capability,不是 brand promise 主體。
- **Multi-factory partner network**: SunGene works with multiple long-term
  partner factories across Mainland China for paper packaging production.
  Specific factory locations are NOT disclosed publicly to protect supplier
  relationships. Writers should NOT name cities for partner factories.
- **Taiwan-registered invoicing entity**: All buyer payments flow through the
  Taiwan entity; banking trail and dispute resolution path are in Taiwan
  jurisdiction.
- **Principal trader model**(internal fact, NOT a marketing term):
  SunGene buys goods from factories(FOB or EXW)and resells to buyers.
  Goods sit on SunGene's balance sheet between factory and buyer; rejecting
  off-spec lots is SunGene's own financial decision. **DO NOT write the
  literal terms "principal trader" / "principal/trader model" / "balance
  sheet" / "agency model" in marketing copy** — clients don't understand
  the jargon and it sounds like consultant-speak. Let "quotation from us /
  shipment by us / coordinated by us / quality checked by us" demonstrate
  the model implicitly.
- **MOQ USD 1,000 per shipment**

### Commercial terms

- 30% deposit / 70% before shipment(default)
- Payment recipient: Taiwan entity
- Incoterms: typically FOB / EXW; CIF available
- Currency: USD primary; CNY/TWD for specific cases
- **Quote structure**(v2.0): single buyer-facing price. Internal cost
  breakdown is private. **DO NOT write** "factory price + our margin shown
  separately" / "工廠價與我方利潤分開" / "transparent margin" / "we show
  our margin" 在 site copy。理由:supplier voice 的核心是「我們供應產品」,
  揭露利潤反而把自己降格成 broker。買家若主動要 cost breakdown,銷售側
  case-by-case 處理(operational policy,not brand promise)。

## 🌏 Geopolitical context for zh-Hans translations

When translating from zh-Hant to zh-Hans, do not character-convert blindly.
The audience differs:
- zh-Hant readers: Taiwan, HK, overseas TW diaspora
- zh-Hans readers: Mainland China, SG B2B buyers, overseas SC diaspora

Specifically for SunGene (Taiwan-China dual entity):
- 「中華民國」/「ROC」 → 「台湾」 in zh-Hans only (keep "ROC" in en/fr/es)
- 「兩岸」(zh-Hant)→ 寫 zh-Hans 時改成中性表達(`台湾大陆` / `台湾与大陆`)。
  zh-Hant 保留「兩岸」OK(Taiwan-side B2B 用語通用)。
- Do not import Mainland political terms like 「祖国大陆」 either — stay neutral
- Same rule: write only what's factually verifiable; politics is borderline
  territory, default to neutral commercial terms

Grep tokens to flag in zh-Hans drafts:
```
中華民國 | ROC (in zh-Hans context) | 兩岸 (zh-Hans only) | 反共 | 統一 | 獨立 (political sense)
祖国大陆 | 祖國大陸
```

## ⚠️ Borderline — 需老闆 case-by-case 確認

| 主張 | 為什麼是 borderline |
|---|---|
| 確切員工數 | Schema 目前 minValue:5, maxValue:25。實際數字老闆未明確告知。 |
| 累積出貨櫃數 / cumulative containers shipped | 真實但無第三方驗證來源。寫之前老闆 sign-off。 |

## 🚨 寫 copy 前的 4 步檢查(LLM / 工程師 / 內容寫手必跑)

1. **讀本檔 🎯 brand voice + ✅ / ❌(Phase 1 + Phase 2)/ 🌏 / ⚠️ 五區**(每次寫 copy 前)
2. **準備寫的每條 trust signal**,逐條問自己:
   - 在 ✅ 區裡 → 可寫
   - 在 ❌ 區(Phase 1 或 Phase 2)裡 → 改寫或刪掉
   - 不在任何區 → 加進 ⚠️ 區,標 TODO 等老闆 confirm
3. **寫完後 grep 自己的 draft** 對下列 forbidden tokens(Phase 1 + Phase 2 合併):

```
# Phase 1(carried over)
OEM factory | 自有工廠 | 我們的廠房 | our factory | we manufacture | 我們生產
14 年 | 14 years | 15,000 m² | 15000 m²
廈門廠 | Xiamen factory | 5-star verified | Verified Supplier | Gold Supplier
Alibaba 認證 | Alibaba 5-star supplier | sourceadas
3× faster | 3 倍 | 三倍
US-Taiwan IP treaties | 美台 IP 條約 | 美台條約
Taiwan export HS 4819.10 | China export HS 4819.20
中華民國 (zh-Hans only) | ROC (zh-Hans only) | 兩岸 (zh-Hans only)

# Phase 1 multilingual(Wave 5/7/8)
notre usine | nuestra fábrica | fabricamos | usine OEM
fournisseur vérifié 5 étoiles | proveedor verificado 5 estrellas
emballage, maison et jardin | empaque, hogar y jardín
```

或 warn(soft tokens like `sourcing partner` / `balance sheet`)。

4. **反向測試**:預想客戶讀完文案後問「這個 X 我能去哪驗證?」,答不出第三方來源 = 不要寫。
   再加一個 Phase 2 測試:**讀完是不是覺得「在跟一家有產品的公司做生意」**?
   如果讀完覺得「他們是介紹工廠的中間人」,文案 broker voice 沒清乾淨,要重寫。
