fix: round-18 — generateStaticParams unblocks ISR for non-list pages

R17 added `experimental.isrMemoryCacheSize: 0` which forces the on-disk cache
tier. That helps pages that have generateStaticParams (their HTML lands on
disk at build). But pages WITHOUT generateStaticParams still go SSR-on-demand
in cluster mode (in-memory cache disabled, no on-disk variant produced) — so
/en homepage, /en/about, /en/contact, /[lang]/[compareSlug], and all non-en
langs were still MISS-MISS-MISS-MISS after R17.

Auditor caught it: "8 pages × 4 hits each, /en was 4 × MISS."

#1 generateStaticParams declared in app/[lang]/layout.tsx
   Returns [{lang:'en'},{lang:'zh'},{lang:'cn'},{lang:'fr'},{lang:'es'}].
   Because every leaf page below uses `export const dynamic = 'force-static'`,
   declaring lang params at the parent layout makes Next prerender 5
   variants of every page at build time. They land on disk and are served
   directly by both PM2 workers.

   Verified after deploy:
     /en: HIT HIT HIT HIT ✓
     /en/about, /en/contact, /en/vs-alibaba-direct, /zh, /fr, /es:
        MISS HIT HIT HIT — cold first request after fresh deploy
        (expected ISR warm-up), then HIT for every subsequent request
   Build output confirmed `● /[lang] (SSG)` + per-lang segments produced.

#2 /en homepage meta_desc 191 → 148ch
   Source was lib/i18n.ts meta_home_desc (en), not layout.tsx generateMetadata.
   Trimmed the redundant "Sourcing partner with teams in Taichung and
   Xiamen." prefix and "verified" qualifier; kept the key signal phrases
   (Taiwan + China sourcing, vetted factories, on-site QC, Alibaba 5-star,
   MOQ USD 1,000). Now within Google SERP display window.

#3 /sourcing en title 66 → 53ch
   "How SunGene sources direct from Taiwan + China factories" (56ch +
   " | SunGene" = 66ch — at SERP cutoff boundary)
   → "SunGene direct sourcing from Taiwan + China" (43ch +
   " | SunGene" = 53ch — well inside window)

#4 vs-sourcing-agent zh + cn whenUs deepened (was lighter than vs-alibaba)
   Auditor: zh/cn vs-agent at 62% of en while vs-alibaba at 73%. Rebalanced.
   6 bullets → 7 bullets per lang, each ~2× original length. Added explicit
   reasoning about:
   - 「法律意義的所有權轉移」(FOB/EXW invoice naming SunGene as buyer)
   - 「為什麼我們不會說對方不方便給發票」(invoices ARE in our hands)
   - 「微信號 vs 台灣司法」(jurisdiction contrast)
   - 「margin 揭露的驗證方式」(buyer can cross-check factory invoice
     vs our quote-line "factory price")
